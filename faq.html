What is the purpose of Phone Formatter?

Phone Formatter helps you to store your phone numbers on your android device in a common format. For example, you might have the following numbers saved on your device:
  • 0049 30 65476
  • 030 17345
  • +49 (30) 12245
Phone Formatter formats them nicely like
  • +49 30 65476
  • +49 30 17345
  • +49 30 12245
In order to do so, Phone Formatter scans your contact numbers and proposes a standard format for it. You can accept and store the numbers in the proposed format.

What format does Phone Formatter use?

The rules for building phonenumbers out of country code, region code and number vary from country to country and are full of exceptions. Therefor, Phone Formatter uses the the libphonenumber library, which has all this rules build in.

Which country code is added by default?

Your default country.
